# VU SR Revision Commission

![VU SR Revision Commission](/img/dariniai/Revizija.jpg){.rounded-sm}

The Revision Commission is a supervisory body responsible for auditing the finances and operations of VU SR’s structural units. The Commission consists of five members, elected at the VU SR Annual Reporting-Electing Conference.

During the reporting period, the Revision Commission consisted of:

- Gabija Pakatiliūtė (Chairperson until December)
- Egmontas Taraila (Chairperson since December)
- Ainetė Sabaliauskaitė
- Lukas Krasauskas
- Viktorija Jonuškaitė

In 2025–2026, the Revision Commission updated the VU SR performance evaluation methodology and conducted an evaluation of the Organization’s activities in accordance with it. During the reporting period, five closed meetings of the Revision Commission were held. In addition, approximately 100 individual meetings were held, and three anonymous surveys were conducted among the audited structural units of VU SR.

During the meetings, an assessment was made of whether VU SR (U), the VU SR Parliament, the VU SR Council, the VU SR ISF, student initiatives, and other structural units of the Organization meet the qualitative and quantitative criteria. Information was collected not only during meetings with the units under review, but also by participating in VU SR Parliament and/or VU SR Council meetings and various Organization events.

The Revision Commission also provided clarifications on documents regulating activities and advised VU SR members on various issues — 5 clarifications were issued. During the reporting period, the Revision Commission presented an interim activity report at an open meeting of the VU SR Parliament.

The Revision Commission submits the report on the evaluation of the organization’s activities during the VU SR reporting and election conference (the evaluation report is available [here](https://www.vusa.lt/lt/dokumentai?contentTypes%5B%5D=Veiklos+ataskaitos)).
