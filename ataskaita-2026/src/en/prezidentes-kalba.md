# President's Speech

![Klėja Merčaitytė](../public/img/people/kleja-mercaityte.jpg)

Dear students, dear members of the Organization,

The 35th year of the Students' Representation has been full of change. The main directions and work of this year focused on individual studies, the creation and strengthening of initiatives, students with disabilities, and resilience to external threats.

We actively contributed to shaping the new Government program and its implementation plan, submitted proposals, and joined different working groups on issues important to students.

We worked closely with higher education institutions on student involvement in research activities, prevention of academic ethics violations, and the development of the social dimension in higher education. We built dialogue within the international ARQUS alliance and exchanged good practices with student unions abroad. We worked on updating the Organization's documents, Style Guide, Integration and planning processes, established the Student Initiatives Center, and systematically strengthened the competencies of Organization members.

I can confidently say that VU SR is strong, visible, and heard.

We will enter the upcoming academic year with newly approved strategic projects that touch every member of our community. I am proud of the Organization, our shared involvement, and our strong partnerships.

I invite you to explore all the work completed during the year in the VU SR 2025–2026 report. Thank you sincerely for this year, and let us continue working "United for the future of students!"

**Klėja Merčaitytė**

VU SR President
